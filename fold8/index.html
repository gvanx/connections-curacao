Pre-order FAQ

How much do I pay now?

A 50% deposit of your selected variant's price, paid securely via Sentoo. The remaining 50% is paid when you collect the phone in store.

Is my deposit refundable?

If we can't fulfill your pre-order, your deposit is fully refunded. For change-of-mind cancellations, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll walk you through the options.

Can I change color or storage later?

Yes — as long as the variant is still available, just message us on WhatsApp and we'll update your reservation.
